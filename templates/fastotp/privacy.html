Privacy Policy

We believe privacy is a right, not a feature. Here's exactly what data we collect, why, and how we protect it.

1. Data We Collect

We collect only what's strictly necessary to provide the FastOTP service:

  • Account data: Name, email address, company name, WhatsApp number for identity verification.
  • Delivery logs: Phone numbers or emails you send OTPs to, the delivery status, and latency — necessary to provide the service and troubleshoot issues.
  • Billing data: Payment method information is handled entirely by Paystack or Flutterwave. We never store card numbers.
  • Usage data: API request logs, IP addresses, and user agents for security and rate limiting.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to:

  • Deliver OTPs you initiate through the API
  • Generate your usage reports and billing statements
  • Detect and prevent abuse of the platform
  • Send transactional emails (password reset, low credit alerts)

We do not sell, rent, or trade your data to any third party.

3. Data Retention

OTP delivery logs are retained for 90 days by default for debugging and analytics. You may request earlier deletion via our support channel. Account data is retained until you delete your account.

4. Your Rights

Under applicable data protection laws (NDPR, GDPR), you have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data. Contact privacy@fastotp.co to exercise these rights.

5. Security

All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256). OTPs are stored as one-way hashes — we cannot recover the original OTP. API keys are generated with 256 bits of entropy.

6. Contact

For privacy concerns: privacy@fastotp.co
